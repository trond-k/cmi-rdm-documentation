# RDM Hub

Practical guidance on managing research data throughout the research lifecycle — from planning through to archiving and deletion.

This site is organised around three phases of a research project:

- **[Before](before/index.md)** — Plan your data management before data collection begins.
- **[During](during/index.md)** — Keep data organised, documented, and secure while research is active.
- **[After](after/index.md)** — Clean, archive, share, or delete data when a project wraps up.

Each section is self-contained. Jump directly to the phase you are in.

!!! tip "New to research data management?"
    Start with the [data management plan](before/dmp.md) page — many funders now require one, and working through it early saves time later.
