# Before: plan your data management

Good data management starts before you collect a single data point. The planning phase is where you make decisions about storage, ethics, and data sharing that will shape the rest of the project.

## In this section

- [Data management plans](dmp.md) — Write a plan that satisfies funder requirements and actually helps your project.
- [Ethics and approvals](ethics.md) — Navigate ethics review, informed consent, and Norwegian privacy regulations.
- [Storage](storage.md) — Choose where and how to store your data securely.
- [Partnerships](partnerships.md) — Set up data-sharing agreements for cross-border and multi-institution collaborations.
